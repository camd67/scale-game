You enter a strange room, full of a talking demon's scale, enchanted tables, strange potions, and floating tiny objects. Welcome to the demon's scales.


Trapped in this room with no exits, all you can hope to do is appease the demon's desire to... balance things on a his scales?

Survive the demon's onslaught of puzzle after puzzle, balancing the scales so they come out even. But is it actually your life that's hanging in the balance?

Controls

  • Mouse to interact with objects on the left side of the scale
  • Move objects onto the scale
  • Click the lock on the scale to submit your guess

Made in 96 hours for the GMTK 2024 game jam, with three game devs, all in Godot.
